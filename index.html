How to Play

  • Click and drag from one matching glyph to another to create a link.
  • Your goal is to fill the entire board with links.
  • Links cannot cross each other or go through other glyphs.
  • Think spatially and plan your paths carefully!

About the Game

GlyphLink is a challenging puzzle game that tests your spatial reasoning. Connect the ancient glyphs and solve intricate puzzles that become more complex as you progress. Relax, focus, and find the flow.
